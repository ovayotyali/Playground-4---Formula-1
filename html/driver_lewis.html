Biography

Lewis Hamilton is one of the most successful drivers in Formula 1 history, known for his raw speed, racecraft, and consistency. He debuted in 2007 with McLaren and won his first championship in 2008. His dominance in the hybrid era with Mercedes earned him six more titles.
